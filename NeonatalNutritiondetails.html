    Total Neonatal Nutrition

    Project Summary

    • Tech: C#, VB, Excel, SQL
    • Libraries: Office Interop
    • Techniques: System design, Macros, Conditional formatting, Named ranges
    • Start date: January, 2018

    Total Neonatal Nutrition

    As a dietitian, I was promoted to handle nutrition care for premature infants in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit. With a lack of professional experience in this specialty, I knew I needed to develop something to help me tackle the learning curve of this calculation-intensive discipline.

    Over a year and a half, I compiled a number of different functions into the system that covered every aspect of nutritional care for infants and unit management. I recorded a series of videos to capture the features and provide a tutorial for others in the region to use.

    This was the project that inspired me to learn coding more formally. I began incorporating Visual Basic into functions and I was astounded by the possibilities of what could be achieve with code.
